# Luo Kai: Open World — World Factions

## Purpose
Define the social powers that shape the world so the game feels political, local, and lived-in.

## Faction structure
The world should be organized by influence, not just combat strength.

### 1. Village households
- ordinary people
- value stability, food, safety, and tradition
- live closest to Spirit Forest
- interact mostly through practical requests

### 2. Shrine keepers
- manage ritual sites and spiritual balance
- know offerings, chants, and local taboos
- shape how the village relates to qi
- often guide cultivation beginners

### 3. Road wardens
- protect roads, crossings, and travel posts
- care about bandits, beasts, and weather
- give escort and safety requests
- sit between village life and outer danger

### 4. Hunter circles
- track beasts, spirits, and hidden movement
- know the edges of each zone
- often know the land better than officials
- give clear-out and tracking requests

### 5. Artisan and forge groups
- make weapons, tools, talismans, and practical gear
- care about material supply and reputation
- sit near production spaces
- connect economy to progression

### 6. Sect outer influence
- formal cultivation institutions
- care about rank, order, and access
- appear strongest in Jade Sect Outer Grounds
- control some roads, resources, and training spaces

### 7. Wandering cultivators
- not fully tied to any one place
- carry rumors, relic knowledge, and odd requests
- may be helpful, dangerous, or both
- often appear at camps, ruins, or road stops

## Faction behavior
Each faction should behave differently in the world.
- villagers worry about survival
- shrine keepers worry about balance
- wardens worry about safety
- hunters worry about signs and tracks
- artisans worry about materials
- sect members worry about order
- wanderers worry about freedom, gain, or mystery

## Reputation effects
The player’s actions should change how factions see them.
- help the shrine and elders trust you
- protect roads and wardens trust you
- bring materials and artisans trust you
- prove strength and sect members notice you
- help hunters and they share hidden paths

## Regional influence
- **Spirit Forest**: villagers, shrine keepers, hunters
- **Mist Ridge**: wardens, hunters, travelers, wandering cultivators
- **Jade Sect Outer Grounds**: sect influence, artisans, students, guards
- **Soul Vale**: rare specialists, lore keepers, unstable wanderers
- **Ascension Peak**: ritual authorities, high cultivators, trial guardians

## Why it matters
Factions make the world feel social. They give the player a reason to belong, to be tolerated, to be trusted, or to be watched.
